TO: HR Director
FROM: Data Science Intern
SUBJECT: Key Findings on Employee Attrition

Based on our analysis of employee data, here are the key findings:

1. TOP 3 FACTORS PREDICTING ATTRITION:
   - Jobrole Laboratory Technician
   - Overtime Yes
   - Businesstravel Travel Frequently

2. HIGHEST RISK DEPARTMENTS:
   - Sales department has the highest attrition rate at 20.6%
   - Sales Representative role has the highest attrition at 39.8%

3. SALARY INSIGHTS:
   - Employees who left earned $2,045.65 less on average
   - Salary alone does not explain attrition

4. CONCRETE HR RECOMMENDATIONS:
   a) Conduct stay interviews with employees working overtime
   b) Target retention efforts on Sales department
   c) Review compensation for WorkLifeBalance rating 1 employees
   d) Implement mentorship programs at 3-5 year tenure milestones

5. MODEL LIMITATIONS:
   - Predicts based on historical patterns, not guaranteed future
   - Cannot capture personal reasons like family relocation
   - Should be used as screening tool, not sole basis for decisions
